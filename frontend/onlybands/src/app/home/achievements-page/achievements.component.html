Jashin - BITS pilani, Dubai 2022

Our very first experience on a big stage with a completely random crowd of people from the hosting university, BITS pilani. Playing three songs for the battle of the bands that night. Representing our home university BathSpa RAK.

Seeking more thrill and experience, we started looking for more opportunities outside our university’s scope and dipped our toes into a well known Filipino seasonal event in Muraqabat, Dubai. Playing simple modern and classic opm pop taught us valuable lessons from the OG’s who guided us throughout.
